# Assurance Journey

**Audience:** Security, privacy, risk, audit, and conformity-readiness teams

## Learning objective

Complete the journey with a reviewable **control applicability and assurance evidence matrix**.

## Sequence

1. Review governance-to-control mapping.
2. Identify applicable controls and invalidation triggers.
3. Verify evidence completeness and traceability.

## Decision checkpoint

For each material decision, identify the authority source, delegated decision owner, enforcement mechanism, evidence output, and supersession trigger.

## Produce this artifact

<div class="learning-output">Control applicability and assurance evidence matrix with named owners, source references, review status, and outstanding risks.</div>

## Completion criteria

- the artifact exists in a durable repository location;
- each material item has an accountable owner;
- claims are traceable to authoritative inputs;
- enforcement can be tested; and
- unresolved gaps are recorded rather than implied away.
