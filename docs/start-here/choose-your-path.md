# Choose Your Path

Select the journey that corresponds to the decision or artifact you must produce.

| Role | Journey | Completion artifact |
|---|---|---|
| Policy or programme leadership | [Executive briefing](../learning/executive-briefing/) | authority and accountability register |
| Enterprise or solution architect | [Architect journey](../learning/architect-path/) | architecture decision and trust-boundary pack |
| Delivery or integration team | [Implementer journey](../learning/implementer-path/) | implementation backlog and evidence plan |
| Security, privacy, or assurance | [Assurance journey](../learning/assurance-path/) | control applicability and evidence matrix |
| Repository or governance maintainer | [Maintainer journey](../learning/maintainer-path/) | drift assessment and traceable update |

{: .decision }
> Choose the journey according to the artifact you are accountable for producing, not merely your job title.
