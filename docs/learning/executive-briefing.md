# Executive Briefing

**Audience:** Policy and programme leaders

## Learning objective

Complete the journey with a reviewable **authority and accountability register**.

## Sequence

1. Read the legal baseline and ARF explanation.
2. Identify decisions retained locally.
3. Assign owners for synchronization, implementation, assurance, and escalation.

## Decision checkpoint

For each material decision, identify the authority source, delegated decision owner, enforcement mechanism, evidence output, and supersession trigger.

## Produce this artifact

<div class="learning-output">Authority and accountability register with named owners, source references, review status, and outstanding risks.</div>

## Completion criteria

- the artifact exists in a durable repository location;
- each material item has an accountable owner;
- claims are traceable to authoritative inputs;
- enforcement can be tested; and
- unresolved gaps are recorded rather than implied away.
